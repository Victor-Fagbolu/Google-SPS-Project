Background

As far as backgrounds go, mine is quite complex. For starters, my first name is Oluwasegun, I go by Victor because many people find it difficult to pronounce it. I was born in Lagos, Nigeria, but have lived in the United States most of my life. I currently live in Missouri City. My family moved often, and due to this, I attended three different High Schools. This experience helped me to be able to better adapt to new environments and situations.

School

I currently a rising junior at Prairie View A&M University in Prairie View, Texas. I major in computer science. My reson for choosing computer science is quite strange and some may say it's reckless. Growing up, I was always good at math, and for most of high school, I thought I would major in math. It wasn't until I was completing the college application that I change my mind and decided to go with computer science. In fact, I applied to a different school previously and was going to major in math, but decided not to go to that school for a seprate reason.

As a result of that last minute decision I find myself at a place right now in my life where I know for a certain that I could not be happier doing anything else. I completely fell in love with coding and computer science in general. The only thing I would have done differently is to begin studying computer science in highschool.
